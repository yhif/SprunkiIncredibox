Introducing Gray: The Cool Cat of the Incredibox Universe

Gray, referred to as Gray Sprunki in Sprunki Incredibox and highlighted in Colorbox V10, is the relaxed, feline-themed character of the Incredibox universe. With his cool attitude and smooth bass sounds, Gray enhances depth and rhythm in any musical creation across various game versions.

Comparison of Versions

Gray Sprunki

In Sprunki Incredibox, Gray Sprunki is celebrated for his distinct bass tones and cool cat aesthetic. He's a favorite among players for laying the groundwork for sprunky beats.

Gray Incredibox

The iconic Gray Incredibox character retains his essence throughout various Incredibox versions, consistently delivering that essential bass component to compositions.

Gray Colorbox V10
