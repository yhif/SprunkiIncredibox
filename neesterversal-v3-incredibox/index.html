Overview of Neesterversal v3 Incredibox

Neesterversal v3 is a unique mode that broadens the Incredibox universe. It showcases 20 different Neester characters, each with their own distinct sounds and styles.

How to Play:

  • For an enhanced experience, please use headphones!
  • Drag and drop an icon onto a character to make them sing.
  • Drag a character down to make them stop singing.

Welcome to the third edition of the Neesterversal, featuring 20 different Neesters from 20 unique mods! There’s also some lore hidden in the game, so feel free to explore it!
